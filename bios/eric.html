Eric Li


 		Instrument: Trombone
Bio:
Eric is a Junior in Wharton and plays trombone for the PGCB. If you ever stop by a PGCB gig, it would be hard not to notice Eric's low, sultry trombone tones as he slips and slides around stage. A bonafide, trombone prodigy, Eric is a staple of the PGCB horn section.
